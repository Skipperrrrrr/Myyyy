To the love of my life, Lorrie —

I don’t know when exactly it happened, but somewhere between those long walks, the times you laughed at my foolish jokes, and the days you said “you haven’t texted me all day,” I fell. I didn’t just like you. I didn’t just love you. I chose you.

I’ve watched you grow, pray, love deeply, and serve with your whole heart. I’ve sat next to you in moments that were light, and others that felt heavy. And each time, I felt like I was where I was meant to be.

You are not just beautiful. You are kind in the loudest and softest ways. You’ve made me a better man — and I’ve never stopped being amazed that I get to be loved by you.

Sometimes I imagine our future — not as some perfect Instagram life, but as real as burnt rice, sleepy Sundays, early morning devotions with sleepy-eyed kids, and laughter echoing through a home we built together. I want that with you.

You once asked me if I was sure.

Lorrie… I am.

Will you marry me?
